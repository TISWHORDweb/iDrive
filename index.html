Transparent Pricing

Price Summary

WiDrive offers flexible and affordable pricing options for both intra-state and inter-state trips. Choose the payment method that works best for you.

Intra-State Payment System

We operate a distance-based and time-based pricing system for trips within the same state or city.

  • Distance-Based: The app calculates the distance and provides a fair price.
  • Time-Based: Choose hourly, daily, or weekly pricing based on your needs.
  • Pay via wallet or saved debit card. (We recommend the wallet option for better expense management.)

Inter-State Payment System

For trips between states, we offer a fixed and affordable pricing system.

  • Wallet Payment Only: Ensures secure and hassle-free transactions.
  • Fixed Pricing: No extra charges for traffic delays or time wastage.
  • Your driver will ensure a safe and timely journey to your destination.

Transparent Policies

Refund/Cancellation Policy

We strive to provide fair and transparent policies for both drivers and clients. Here's how refunds and cancellations are handled.

Driver Cancellation

If a driver cancels a ride, an investigation will be carried out. If the driver is found culpable, a full refund will be made, and the driver will be sanctioned (which may include a lifetime ban on the app).

Client Cancellation

Before Departure: No refund is required as the wallet account is not charged.

During Trip: If the car breaks down or the client cancels midway, only 20% of the total sum will be withheld.

After Departure: If the driver has left the state or city, the trip will be charged at full cost with no refunds.
