WHO WE ARE

WiDrive to Save You Stress!

Founded on March 4, 2020, WiDrive is a tech-driven mobility solution revolutionizing personal transportation. We empower clients to enjoy their vehicles without the hassle of driving. Whether you're fatigued, celebrating, or simply prefer to relax, our professional drivers ensure you arrive safely and comfortably.

Our Mission

To redefine personal transportation by providing secure, reliable, and convenient driver services that enhance our clients' quality of life.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading on-demand driver service, setting the standard for safety, convenience, and customer satisfaction in personal transportation.

Safety First

All drivers undergo 5-stage verification including background checks, KYC validation, and defensive driving certification.

Flexible Services

Customizable plans from 1-hour trips to weekly engagements. No long-term commitments - use only when needed.

Your Car, Your Rules

Travel in the comfort of your own vehicle while we handle the driving. Maintain your preferred climate, music, and seating.

Nationwide Coverage

Available in all major cities with expanding coverage. Interstate trips accommodated with advance notice.

WiDrive helps car owners find professional drivers for safe and comfortable trips. Whether for daily commutes, road trips, or special events, we've got you covered.
